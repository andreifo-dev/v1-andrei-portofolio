02.About

Introduction

Hi, my name is Andrei and I am a web developer and programmer. I have a strong passion for building and creating websites and applications. I love the feeling of satisfaction that comes with seeing my code come to life and function as intended. In my free time, you can often find me tinkering with new technologies and techniques in order to constantly improve my skills and stay up-to-date.



Back in the days

I was introduced to computers in middle school and immediately became fascinated by the technology. Despite initial hesitation, I began to experiment with different programs and eventually taught myself how to code.

Initial steps towards programming

My first steps in programming began in high school where I learned C++. After developing a strong foundation in C++, I decided to explore other programming languages on my own. Later on I began by teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through online platforms such as Scrimba, Codecademy, and freeCodeCamp. These resources provided me with the knowledge and skills to continue learning and growing as a programmer. As I worked through the lessons and projects on these platforms, I gained confidence in my ability to code and became increasingly motivated to continue learning and improving my skills.



Developers After Party

In the end of 2022, I teamed up with a good friend of mine to start a project we call Developers After Party. We're both passionate about using our skills to help others, and we believe that we can make a difference by assisting small businesses and professionals individuals to establish a strong online presence. Our aim is to create designs that not only look good but also represent their brand and deliver their message effectively. We believe that a website should be more than just a digital storefront - it should be a reflection of the business or professional it represents.


But that's not all - as part of this project, I'm also writing a tech-related newsletter. I've always been a curious person, and I love sharing my knowledge with others. So, if you're curious like me, you should definitely check out my newsletter! It's filled with all kinds of interesting topics, and I think you'll really enjoy it. Go ahead and check it out - Just click here!


This portfolio demonstrate the skills and knowledge I have acquired as a programmer. As I continue to learn and grow in my field, I will add new projects to this portfolio to showcase my progress and development. I am always striving to improve my skills and expand my knowledge, and this portfolio serves as a reflection of that journey.
